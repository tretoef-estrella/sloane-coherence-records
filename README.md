# sloane-coherence-records
Two world records in the Grassmannian coherence packing problem (d=4, n=64) hlc, established May 12, 2026 from a Mac M2 laptop. Both records verified byte-exact by 5 independent kernels. Previous record (Cohn et al.) held for 14+ years. Beaten twice in one day. Records, paper, and verifier included.
